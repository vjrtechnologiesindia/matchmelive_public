Child Safety Standards

MatchMeLive is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users, especially children. We have a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Prohibited Conduct

  • Grooming or soliciting minors.
  • Sharing or distributing child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Any form of predatory or abusive behavior toward minors.

Reporting Mechanism

Users can report any concerns related to CSAE directly from the app using the in-app reporting feature. All reports are reviewed promptly, and appropriate action is taken immediately.

Handling of CSAM

Any detected CSAM is removed immediately. We cooperate fully with law enforcement authorities to address illegal content and abusive behavior.

Legal Compliance

We comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding child protection, including international standards for child safety.
